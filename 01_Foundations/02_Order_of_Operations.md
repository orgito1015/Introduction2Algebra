## 📖 Key Concepts

**PEMDAS** (US) / **BODMAS** (UK) is the agreed-upon order to evaluate expressions:

| Step | Name | Examples |
|------|------|---------|
| 1 | **P**arentheses / **B**rackets | `(3 + 2)` |
| 2 | **E**xponents / **O**rders | `2³`, `√4` |
| 3 | **M**ultiplication & **D**ivision | `4 × 2`, `8 ÷ 2` (left to right) |
| 4 | **A**ddition & **S**ubtraction | `5 + 3`, `9 - 1` (left to right) |

> ⚠️ Multiplication and division have equal priority — evaluate **left to right**.  
> ⚠️ Addition and subtraction have equal priority — evaluate **left to right**.

## ✏️ Worked Examples

### Example 1

**Problem:** Evaluate `3 + 6 × 2`.

**Solution:**  
1. Multiplication first: `6 × 2 = 12`  
2. Addition: `3 + 12 = 15`  
**Answer: 15**

---

### Example 2

**Problem:** Evaluate `(2 + 3)² - 4 ÷ 2`.

**Solution:**  
1. Parentheses: `(2 + 3) = 5`  
2. Exponent: `5² = 25`  
3. Division: `4 ÷ 2 = 2`  
4. Subtraction: `25 - 2 = 23`  
**Answer: 23**

## 🧪 Practice Problems

1. Evaluate `8 ÷ 2 + 3 × 4`.
2. Evaluate `(5 - 2)³ + 6 ÷ 3`.
3. Evaluate `2 + 3 × (8 - 5)² ÷ 9`.

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. `8 ÷ 2 + 3 × 4 = 4 + 12 = 16`
2. `(5 - 2)³ + 6 ÷ 3 = 27 + 2 = 29`
3. `2 + 3 × (8 - 5)² ÷ 9 = 2 + 3 × 9 ÷ 9 = 2 + 3 = 5`
