## 📖 Key Concepts

**Goal:** isolate the variable on one side of the equation.

**Properties used:**
- **Addition Property of Equality** — add the same value to both sides.
- **Subtraction Property of Equality** — subtract the same value from both sides.
- **Multiplication Property of Equality** — multiply both sides by the same value.
- **Division Property of Equality** — divide both sides by the same non-zero value.

### General Strategy

1. Simplify each side (distribute, combine like terms).
2. Move variable terms to one side.
3. Move constant terms to the other side.
4. Divide by the coefficient of the variable.
5. Check your solution by substituting back.

## ✏️ Worked Examples

### Example 1 — One-step

**Problem:** Solve `x - 4 = 9`.

**Solution:** Add 4 to both sides: `x = 13`

---

### Example 2 — Two-step

**Problem:** Solve `3x + 5 = 20`.

**Solution:**  
Subtract 5: `3x = 15`  
Divide by 3: `x = 5`

---

### Example 3 — Variables on both sides

**Problem:** Solve `4x - 3 = 2x + 7`.

**Solution:**  
Subtract `2x`: `2x - 3 = 7`  
Add 3: `2x = 10`  
Divide by 2: `x = 5`
