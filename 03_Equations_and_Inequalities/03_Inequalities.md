## 📖 Key Concepts

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `>` | Greater than |
| `<` | Less than |
| `≥` | Greater than or equal to |
| `≤` | Less than or equal to |

**Key rule:** When multiplying or dividing both sides of an inequality by a **negative** number, **flip the inequality sign**.

## ✏️ Worked Examples

### Example 1

**Problem:** Solve and graph `2x - 3 > 7`.

**Solution:**  
Add 3: `2x > 10`  
Divide by 2: `x > 5`  
Graph: open circle at 5, arrow pointing right.

---

### Example 2 — Flip the sign

**Problem:** Solve `-4x ≤ 12`.

**Solution:**  
Divide by `-4` (flip sign): `x ≥ -3`

## 🧪 Practice Problems

1. Solve: `x + 7 < 10`.
2. Solve: `−3x > 9`.
3. Solve and write in interval notation: `5 - 2x ≤ 11`.

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. `x < 3`
2. Divide by `-3`, flip sign: `x < -3`
3. `-2x ≤ 6 → x ≥ -3`, interval notation: `[-3, ∞)`

</details>
